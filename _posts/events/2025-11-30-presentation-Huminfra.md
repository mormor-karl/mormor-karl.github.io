# Presentation at [Huminfra Conference, Stockholm (Sweden),12 November 2025](https://www.huminfra.se/HiC-2025)
A paper written by Elena Volodina, Simon Dobnik, Therese Lindström Tiedemann, Ricardo Muñoz Sánchez, Maria Irena Szawerna, Lisa Södergård & Xuan-Son Vu with the title *Towards shared standards for pseudonymization of research data* was presented by Therese Lindström Tiedemann at the HumInfra conference at Stockholm university, Stockholm (Sweden) in November 2025.

Therese Lindström Tiedemann presented the paper on behalf of the whole Mormor Karl team.
