We are thrilled to announce that the **Joint Workshop on Legal and Ethical Issues in Human Language Technologies (LEGAL2026) and Computational Approaches to Language Data Pseudonymization, Anonymization, De-identification, and Data Privacy (CALD-pseudo 2026)**, co-organized by our Mormor Karl team, will be co-located with [LREC 2026](https://lrec2026.info/)!  

We encourage those interested in submitting to or participaring in the workshop, which will be held in a hybrid format, to check out our [workshop website](https://legal2026.mobileds.de/) and stay tuned for more information there and in the upcoming calls for papers.
